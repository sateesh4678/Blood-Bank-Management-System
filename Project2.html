Our Vision

A Centre of Excellence in Blood Banking, Committed to Safe and High Quality Blood for Thalassemia and Leukemia Children, free of cost , thereby enabling them an opportunity for a normal and holistic life, while canvassing Blood Donation, as only Voluntary, and, at all times, guided by principles of Compassion, Care and uncompromising adherence to Integrity. For 2021, the World Blood Donor Day slogan will be “Give blood and keep the world beating”. The message highlights the essential contribution blood donors make to keeping the world pulsating by saving lives and improving others' health.
